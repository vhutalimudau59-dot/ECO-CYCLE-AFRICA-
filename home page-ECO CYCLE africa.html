welcome to Ecocycle Africa

we are a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental conservation

Eco Cycle Africa was founded in 2010 by a group of environmental enthusiasts in Cape Town. Initially focusing on local beach clean ups, they expanded
to nationwide recycling programs and community education projects. By 2015, they'd partnered with major South African corporations to drive sustainability initiatives.
Today, EcoCycle Africa operates in 5 African countries, promoting eco-friendly practices and reducing waste.
