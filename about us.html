Ecocycle Africa

A site to promote sustainable living in Africa

• Mission statement: Provide a website that will tackle Limited funding for community projects
as well as Low community engagement in recycling initiatives while also Balancing
business partnerships with environmental goals

• Vision statement: to create a leading and user friendly website
that will consider recycling initiatives to the people of various communities with
interactions within the business world while minimizing waste and creating a cleaner, greener Africa.
• Target audience: Environmentally-conscious individuals Community leaders and organisations Businesses looking for sustainable partnerships Government agencies focused on environmental initiatives Educational institutions promoting sustainability
